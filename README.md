
# 📊 Power BI Complete Business Insights 360

## 🌟 Business Context 

AtliQ Hardware is a global company selling computer hardware and peripherals (PCs, printers, mice, etc.) worldwide.  

- **Business Model:** Primarily **B2B**, selling to major retailers (Croma, Best Buy, Staples, Flipkart, Amazon).  
- **Channels:**  
  - **Retailer:** Brick & Mortar and E‑commerce platforms  
  - **Direct:** AtliQ’s own stores (*AtliQ E‑store*, *AtliQ Exclusive*)  
  - **Distributor:** Partners in restricted trade regions  

- **The dashboards in this project simulate these real‑world operations across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive functions.**
---

## 🎯 Project Objective

AtliQ Hardware, a global consumer goods company, faced losses due to outdated Excel‑based analytics. Competitors with modern BI solutions were outperforming them.
This project builds a **Power BI dashboard using 1.8M+ records** from multiple sources (Excel, CSV, MySQL) to deliver actionable insights across Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive functions.

---
## 📊 Key Dashboards 
- **Home Page:** Central navigation hub  
- **Finance View:** Financial planning & profitability tracking  
- **Sales View:** Revenue & market share visibility  
- **Marketing View:** Brand performance & campaign effectiveness  
- **Supply Chain View:** Inventory & logistics optimization  
- **Executive View:** Top‑management KPIs & strategic overview  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills
- **Microsoft Power BI** → ETL, Data Modeling, Visualization, Dashboarding  
- **Data Cleaning:** Power Query  
- **Data Manipulation:** DAX Measures & Columns, Parameters  
- **Data Modeling:** Relationships, normalization, fiscal calendars  
- **Visualization:** Conditional formatting, custom tooltips  
- **Dashboarding:** Filters, slicers, bookmarks, navigation buttons  
- **Publishing:** Power BI Service, report optimization  
- **Documentation:** Structured repo with reports & data model folders  

---

## 📊 Dataset Overview
This project uses the **AtliQ Hardware datasets (gdb041 and gdb056)** , which are **publicly available on Kaggle and GitHub**.  

- **Database Tables:** Customer, Market, Product dimensions; Sales & Forecast fact tables; Costs & Deductions  
- **Excel Files:** Market share, Operational expenses, NS/GM targets  

**Highlights:**  
- **Scale: 1.8M+ rows of monthly sales & forecast data**  
- Integration: Database + Excel sources  
- Coverage: Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, Executive
